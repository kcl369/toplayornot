Advantages of Online Gaming

Promotes Social Interaction

Online gaming creates a sense of community online amongst players, encouraging teamwork and communication.

Jack of all trades

The wide variety of different genres makes it really convenient for players to find games that tap into their true interests and preferences.

Skills Development

Skills development is also a major part of online gaming. The cognitive skills, hand-eye coordination, strategic and critical thinking skills are at the forefront of most online games.

Disadvantages of Online Gaming

Careful not to get lost

The addictive nature of some online games makes it hard for gamers to cope with real-life responsibilities and maintain relationships, while losing any sense of time.

Mind and Body

Repetitive strain injuries also known as RSIs and mental health complications might emerge from heavy gaming habits. Loss of motivation and a general lack of interest in socializing face-to-face is a common issue amongst most gamers.

Where is my piggy bank?

Games that have microtransactions, in-game purchases or monthly subscriptions are often considered as petty expenses. But, when accumulated and paired with a general fear-of-missing-out make up a big sum of money.
